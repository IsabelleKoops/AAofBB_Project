# AAofBB_Project
For the final project of the course Advanced Analysis of Brain and Behaviour at the Univerisity of Groningen. Me and my teammate (mentioned in the notebook) tried to predict gaming disorders and traits based on neural correlates during gaming. We used the data from Li et al. (2025), which is publicly available on OpenNeuro.  

Data: https://openneuro.org/datasets/ds005520/versions/1.0.1

References:
Li, H., Yang, J., Lv, Z., Wan, L., Wang, W., Li, D., Zhou, D., & Kuang, L. (2025). EEG dataset from playing Multiplayer Online Battle Arena games in natural settings. Scientific Data, 12(1), 1129. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-025-05435-5
